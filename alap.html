INNOVATIVE FRENCH CRAFTMANSHIP

Grey Goose Vodka has become known as one of the world’s most iconic vodkas. Grey Goose Vodka is distilled from fine French grapes and at the heart of Grey Goose lies Master Distiller Jean-Sébastien Robicquet and his distillery Maison Villevert. The distillery has been in his family for over 500 years. Find out more about our heritage here.
